In a republic, power is held by the people and their elected representatives with the duty to protect the rights of all. Re:public Ed works to protect public education as an American institution accessible to all and accountable to our people.

Re:public Ed aims to empower the people of Connecticut to shape public education, equalize opportunities for students, and maintain a strong connection between school and community.

Putting the public back into public education!
